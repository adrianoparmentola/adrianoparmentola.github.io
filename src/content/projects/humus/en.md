Hum.us Space Bononia is the flagship case study: a real collective-catering
service analysed and redesigned through the **DMAIC** cycle — the engineering
backbone of Six Sigma — combined with service design tools.

Data was collected through direct observation (Gemba Walk), stakeholder
questionnaires and internal documentation, then structured into a KPI panel
(operations, customer service, financial, personalisation). A direct
competitive benchmark against the main Italian collective-catering players
identified the priority gaps; the **QFD** Cosa/Come matrix and the **Kano**
model translated them into design drivers; **TRIZ** resolved the operating
contradictions into concrete service solutions — from a fast-pickup lane with
modular meal boxes to a single QR touchpoint for menus, allergens and
feedback.

The visual campaign — meal boxes, plates, posters, vouchers, signage —
carries the same identity: *Appetite for Growth*.
