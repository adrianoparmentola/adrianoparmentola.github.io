VAEE is a reusable travel bracelet with a rigid aluminium container, made from
recycled plastic and medical-grade silicone, with a pressure-dispensing system
for creams, serums, and lotions, a flexible design, and a magnetic closure —
built as a practical, sustainable skincare solution for travel.
